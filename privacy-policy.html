Gametime - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Gametime ("we", "our", "the app") is a team management app for sports clubs. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • Team profile data: Jersey number, position, height, weight, phone number, jersey size, ID number, and medical clearance expiry date. This data is provided voluntarily by the user.
  • Messages and content: Chat messages, event RSVPs, and poll votes you create within your teams.
  • Push notification tokens: Device tokens for delivering push notifications, stored only if you enable notifications.
  • Profile photos: Photos you upload are stored in Firebase Storage.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the team management features (chat, calendar, polls, member profiles).
  • To send push notifications about new messages, events, and polls.
  • To display your profile to other members of your team(s).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only visible to members of the team(s) you belong to. We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and storage services.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud) servers. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Access to team data is restricted by Firestore security rules to authorized team members only.

6. Your Rights

  • You can edit or delete your profile data at any time within the app.
  • You can request deletion of your account by contacting us.
  • Team admins can remove members from their team.

7. Children's Privacy

Gametime is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
