# Gametime — Store Listing

## App Name

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Team chat, calendar, polls & squad management for your sports club.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Gametime is the all-in-one team hub for sports clubs. Whether you play basketball, football, handball, or any other team sport — Gametime keeps your team organized and connected.

**Team Chat**
Stay in touch with your teammates. Share updates, coordinate plans, and keep everyone in the loop with real-time messaging.

**Calendar & Events**
Never miss a practice or game. Create matches and training sessions, set locations and times, and track who's coming. Automatic MKOSZ match import available for Hungarian basketball teams.

**Availability & Squad**
Players mark their availability for each event. Coaches and admins can announce the squad for match day — everyone sees the lineup instantly.

**Polls & Voting**
Need to decide on something? Create a poll, let the team vote, and get results in seconds. Perfect for scheduling team dinners, choosing jersey designs, or any team decision.

**Multi-Team Support**
Part of multiple teams? Switch between them seamlessly. Each team has its own chat, calendar, and polls — completely independent.

**Player Profiles**
Keep track of jersey numbers, positions, contact info, medical clearance dates, and more. Admins have a full overview of the entire roster.

**Push Notifications**
Get notified about new messages, upcoming events, and medical clearance expiry reminders.

**Roles & Permissions**
Four roles: Admin, Coach, Player, and Guest. Admins manage the team, coaches help organize, players participate, and guests can observe.

Gametime is free to use. Built for teams, by a team.
